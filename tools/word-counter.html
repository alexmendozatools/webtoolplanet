Word Counter



About this Word Counter

This free Word Counter tool allows you to quickly count the number of words and characters in any text. It is useful for writers, students, bloggers and anyone who needs to meet word limits.

How to use the tool

  1. Paste or type your text in the box.
  2. Click the count button.
  3. The tool will show the total number of words.

Who uses word counters?

Word counters are commonly used when writing essays, blog posts, articles and social media content where word limits or character limits are important.
