Word Counter



About this Word Counter

This free word counter allows you to instantly count the number of words in a text. It is useful for essays, articles, blog posts and social media writing.

How to use this tool

  1. Paste or type your text into the box.
  2. Click the count words button.
  3. The number of words will appear instantly.

Why use a word counter?

Writers and students often need to meet word limits. This tool helps you quickly analyze the length of your text.
