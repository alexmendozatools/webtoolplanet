About this Case Converter

This free Case Converter allows you to instantly convert text between uppercase and lowercase. It is useful for writers, developers and anyone editing text quickly.

How to use this tool

  1. Paste or type your text.
  2. Click uppercase or lowercase.
  3. Your converted text will appear instantly.

Why use a case converter?

Text case tools save time when editing content, coding, writing articles or formatting text documents.
