Age Calculator



About this Age Calculator

This free age calculator allows you to quickly determine your exact age based on your birth date. It calculates the difference between your birth date and today's date.

How to use the tool

  1. Select your birth date.
  2. Click the calculate button.
  3. Your exact age will be displayed instantly.

Why use an age calculator?

Age calculators are useful for forms, official documents, events or simply checking your age in years quickly and accurately.
