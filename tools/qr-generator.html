About this QR Code Generator

This free QR Code Generator allows you to create QR codes instantly from any text, link, or message. QR codes are widely used for sharing websites, contact information, WiFi access, menus, and many other types of data.

How to use this tool

  1. Enter the text or URL you want to convert.
  2. Click the Generate QR button.
  3. Scan the QR code with your smartphone or download it.

Why QR codes are useful

QR codes make it easy to share information quickly without typing long links or text. They are commonly used in marketing, restaurants, tickets, events, and business cards.
