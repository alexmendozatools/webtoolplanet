About this QR Code Generator

This free QR Code Generator allows you to instantly convert text, links or other information into a scannable QR code. QR codes are widely used for sharing links, contact details, Wi-Fi passwords and many other types of information.

How to use this tool

  1. Enter the text or URL you want to convert.
  2. Click the generate button.
  3. Download or scan the QR code with your device.

Why use QR codes?

QR codes make it easy to share information quickly. They are commonly used in marketing, business cards, menus, tickets and many other digital interactions.
